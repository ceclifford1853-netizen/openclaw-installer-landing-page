Privacy Policy

OpenClaw One-Click Installer

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

1. Introduction

OpenClaw Installer Project ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our OpenClaw One-Click Installer software and related services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Installation Information

When you run the OpenClaw One-Click Installer, we may collect:

  • Your Windows system architecture (32-bit or 64-bit)
  • Windows version and build number
  • Installation timestamp
  • Installation success or failure status

2.2 Telemetry Data (Optional)

If you enable telemetry, we may collect:

  • OpenClaw daemon uptime and status
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Performance metrics

Telemetry is disabled by default and requires explicit opt-in.

2.3 GitHub Integration

If you download the installer from GitHub, GitHub may collect analytics data as per their privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Installation Support: To troubleshoot installation issues and improve the installer
  • Product Improvement: To understand how users interact with OpenClaw and identify areas for enhancement
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms
  • Communication: To send you updates, security alerts, and support information

4. Data Retention

We retain collected data for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Specifically:

  • Installation logs: Retained for 90 days
  • Error reports: Retained for 30 days
  • Telemetry data: Retained for 12 months
  • User preferences: Retained until you uninstall OpenClaw

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted to our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access collected data
  • Firewalls: Our servers are protected by firewalls and intrusion detection systems
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party service providers who assist us (e.g., cloud hosting providers)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or court order
  • Business Transfers: If we are acquired or merged, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction
  • Safety: We may disclose information to protect against fraud, security threats, or harm

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

7.1 Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of the data we hold about you.

7.2 Right to Correction

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

7.3 Right to Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.

7.4 Right to Opt-Out

You can disable telemetry and data collection at any time:

  • Run the installer again and select "Minimal Data Collection"
  • Or use: openclaw config set telemetry.enabled false

7.5 Right to Portability

You have the right to request your data in a portable format.

8. Children's Privacy

OpenClaw is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws different from your home country. By using OpenClaw, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of OpenClaw after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@openclawinstaller.com
  • GitHub Issues: Report Privacy Concerns
  • Mailing Address: OpenClaw Installer Project, Privacy Team

12. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - EU
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - USA

  • Other applicable data protection laws
