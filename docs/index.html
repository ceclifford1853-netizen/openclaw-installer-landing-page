OpenClaw Setup FAQ

Why does OpenClaw keep failing on my PC?

Most failures occur because systemd isn't enabled in the Linux subsystem. Our installer automatically injects the correct configuration so your AI daemon starts every time you reboot.

Is this installer safe for beginners?

Yes. We've pre-configured the 2026 security patches and forced the gateway to bind to localhost (127.0.0.1), protecting your data from unauthorized external access.

What's the difference between 64-bit and 32-bit?

64-bit (x64) is recommended for all modern Windows systems and offers better performance. 32-bit (x86) is provided for legacy systems or specific compatibility needs.

Do I need to uninstall anything first?

No. Our installer automatically detects existing installations and upgrades them cleanly without data loss.

How long does installation take?

The entire process—WSL2 setup, Node.js installation, and systemd configuration—completes in approximately 2 minutes on most systems.

What if something goes wrong?

Our installer includes automatic rollback functionality. If any step fails, it safely reverts to your previous state and provides detailed error logs for troubleshooting.
